## Common Mold Triggers in Lavington
- **Complex Roof Structures:** Many large Lavington homes feature complex, multi-pitched roofs. Valleys and hidden gutters can easily block with leaves from mature trees, leading to water overflows into the ceiling and subsequent attic mold.
- **Rising Damp in Older Foundations:** Changes in the water table and degrading damp-proof courses in established homes frequently lead to peeling paint and mold on the lower third of interior walls.
- **En-Suite Bathroom Condensation:** Large, poorly ventilated master bathrooms trap massive amounts of steam, fueling aggressive mold growth on ceilings and high walls.
