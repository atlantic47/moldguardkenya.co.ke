## Common Causes of Mould in Diani
- **Vacation Villa Humidity:** Luxury properties left unoccupied during the low season without active dehumidification often fall victim to the trapped coastal humidity. This results in devastating mould outbreaks ruining expensive fabrics, furniture, and woodwork.
- **Hotel HVAC Systems:** The relentless demand on commercial air conditioning in large resorts leads to massive condensation inside the ductwork. If untreated, toxic mould spores blast into guest rooms, risking health issues and severe reputational damage.
- **Coastal Storm Intrusion:** High winds and driving rain during the monsoon seasons easily breach sliding glass doors and thatch or makuti roofs, causing hidden water damage that rapidly turns into black mould.
