## Why Do Likoni Homes Struggle with Mold?
- **Construction Moisture:** Many new buildings in Likoni are occupied while the concrete and plaster are still emitting thousands of liters of latent moisture. In a sealed home, this leads directly to massive mold blooms on the walls and ceilings within the first year.
- **Sub-Standard Roof Waterproofing:** Heavy coastal rains quickly expose weaknesses in flat roofs or poorly laid iron sheets, sending water directly into the ceiling cavities.
- **Coastal Condensation:** High ambient humidity naturally permeates porous concrete block walls, turning the entire structure into an incubator for green and black mold.
