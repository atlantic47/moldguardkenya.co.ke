## Common Mould Triggers in Roysambu
- **Shared Plumbing Systems:** High-density apartments rely on complex, shared plumbing stacks. A minor leak originating three floors above you can easily trickle down inside the wall shafts, saturating your bathroom drywall and spawning hidden toxic mould.
- **The "Drying Clothes Indoors" Phenomenon:** During Nairobi's cold, wet months, residents are forced to hang laundry inside. The liters of water evaporating off wet clothes get trapped inside the sealed apartments, condensing on cold exterior walls and immediately breeding mould behind wardrobes and beds.
- **Balcony Drainage Issues:** Poorly angled balconies can allow rainwater to pool and seep under sliding doors, ruining hardwood flooring and causing massive fungal blooms underneath the carpet padding.
