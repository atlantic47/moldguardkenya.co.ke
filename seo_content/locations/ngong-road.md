## The Mold Landscape on Ngong Road
- **Commercial HVAC Condensation:** The massive, newly built glass office towers rely entirely on mechanical ventilation. If the AC ducts are not properly insulated, they sweat. This condensation drips onto ceiling tiles or pooling inside ducts, rapidly causing toxic mold that gets blown into the workspace, threatening employee health.
- **Aging Plumbing in Older Residences:** The beautiful, older homes set off Ngong Road often have original, deteriorating plumbing systems. Micro-leaks inside brick walls lead to deep, embedded fungal colonies that cannot be simply wiped away.
- **Construction Site Run-Off:** Continuous heavy construction in the area often alters natural drainage, forcing groundwater to pool against established walls, leading to aggressive rising damp and lower-wall mold.
