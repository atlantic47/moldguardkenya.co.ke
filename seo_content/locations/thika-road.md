## Common Mold Issues on Thika Road
- **Concrete Curing Moisture:** Many new apartment blocks are occupied almost immediately after construction. The thousands of liters of water used to mix the concrete and plaster have not fully evaporated. This trapped moisture, combined with a sealed building, often leads to massive, inexplicable mold outbreaks in the first year of occupancy.
- **Overflowing Gutters and Weak Drainage:** Because of the density of buildings, storm drains are frequently overwhelmed during heavy rains. Water pools around foundation slabs, causing rising damp to aggressively wick up the walls, spawning mold near the floorboards.
- **Window Frame Seepage:** Poorly sealed aluminum or wooden window frames let pounding rainwater seep into the surrounding drywall, creating hidden pockets of moisture that fuel toxic black mold.
