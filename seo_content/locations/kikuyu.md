# Certified Mold Remediation in Kikuyu

Kikuyu features a significantly cooler, greener micro-climate compared to the rest of the Nairobi/Kiambu border. While the lush environment is beautiful, the persistent cold, frequent mists, and heavy rains make Kikuyu properties highly susceptible to lingering dampness. When moisture stays trapped in the walls of a cold house, severe mold outbreaks are guaranteed. **MoldGuard Kenya** is the premier choice for comprehensive mold removal in Kikuyu.

## Why Do Kikuyu Homes Struggle with Mold?
- **Thermal Condensation:** The stark temperature difference between warm interiors (from cooking and heaters) and freezing exterior walls causes intense condensation, feeding black mold around windows and on external-facing bedroom walls.
- **Deep Shade Dampness:** Homes surrounded by mature trees receive very little direct sunlight, preventing external walls and roofs from drying out completely after the rains, leading to pervasive rising damp and roof mold.
