## The Mould Problem in Kasarani
- **Inadequate Bathroom Ventilation:** Many high-rise apartments in Kasarani feature internal bathrooms without windows or effective exhaust fans. The constant steam from showers condensates heavily on walls and ceilings, leading to pervasive green and black mould growth that basic bleach cannot permanently kill.
- **Top Floor Roof Leaks:** Incomplete or degraded waterproofing on the concrete flat roofs of apartment blocks means the "penthouse" units often suffer from severe, hidden ceiling mould during the rainy seasons.
- **Mabati Condensation:** In structures utilizing iron sheets without proper insulation, temperature drops at night cause intense condensation to drip onto the ceiling boards below, rotting the wood and feeding massive fungal colonies.
