Juja is a dynamic town characterized by a huge student population and growing family estates. The high concentration of apartment blocks and hostels poses a unique challenge: high-density living without adequate ventilation almost guarantees severe indoor condensation. ## The Mould Threat in Juja
- **Hostel & Apartment Condensation:** Small living spaces with inadequate exhaust fans trap massive amounts of steam. This creates pervasive mould on bathroom ceilings, behind wardrobes, and around windows.
- **Balcony Leaks:** Pounding rain often exploits cheap sliding door seals, soaking the flooring padding inside apartments and causing hidden mould that ruins the floorboards.
