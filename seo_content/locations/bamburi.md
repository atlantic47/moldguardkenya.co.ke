## The Mold Threat in Bamburi
- **High-Density Apartment Seepage:** A leaking bathroom drain on the third floor can quickly run down the concrete interior shafts of an apartment block, causing massive, hidden mold damage in the ceilings of the units below.
- **Hotel & Hospitality AC Mold:** Constantly running air conditioners create significant internal condensation. If the ductwork is not professionally maintained, black mold colonizes the vents, posing a severe health risk to guests and tenants.
- **Seasonal Torrential Rains:** Sudden coastal downpours frequently overwhelm residential gutters, causing water to pool against ground-floor foundations, resulting in aggressive rising damp and lower-wall mold.
