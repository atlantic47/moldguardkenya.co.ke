## The Mould Threat in Embakasi
- **Flat Roof Leaks:** Many apartment complexes in Embakasi feature flat concrete roofs. If the waterproofing membrane degrades under the harsh Nairobi sun, heavy rains will immediately pool and seep into the top-floor apartments, causing aggressive ceiling mould.
- **Poor Estate Drainage:** Flash floods frequently overwhelm local drainage systems, causing water to pool against ground-floor walls. This leads to rising damp, where moisture is sucked up the walls, breeding fuzzy white and black mould near the skirting boards.
- **Ventilation Issues:** High-density living often results in apartments without adequate cross-ventilation. Simple acts like cooking and showering trap massive amounts of condensation indoors, feeding mould growth in bathrooms and bedrooms.
