# Expert Mould Removal in Kizingo

Kizingo is an established, prestigious neighborhood on Mombasa Island characterized by beautiful historic architecture and mature, shaded streets. However, older buildings often feature aging plumbing and degrading damp-proof courses. When combined with the intense coastal humidity of the island, Kizingo properties frequently suffer from deep structural dampness and stubborn mould outbreaks. **MoldGuard Kenya** is the leading authority on historic and residential mould remediation in Kizingo.

## The Mould Challenge in Kizingo
- **Rising Damp in Older Foundations:** Changes in the island's water table and deteriorating foundational barriers frequently lead to intense capillary action, pulling salty groundwater up into the walls and spawning mould near the floorboards.
- **Leaking Internal Plumbing:** Beautiful old homes often hide degrading iron or clay pipes inside thick structural walls. A slow, hidden leak can rot the interior structure and fuel massive, toxic black mould colonies long before a water stain appears.
- **Dense Coastal Humidity:** Mature, shaded gardens block sea breezes and sunlight, trapping ambient humidity against the exterior walls and encouraging pervasive moss, mildew, and structural mould.
