## Why Mould Removal is Critical in Kenya
Kenya's varying climates—from the humid coastal regions of Mombasa to the cooler, rainy highland areas—create perfect breeding grounds for indoor mould. Mould is not just an unsightly nuisance; it is a biohazard. Prolonged exposure to mould spores can trigger severe respiratory issues, allergies, and asthma. Furthermore, mould attacks the very structure of your home, breaking down organic materials like wood and drywall. Professional mould removal is essential not just for aesthetic reasons, but for protecting your property value and your family's health over the long term.

## Our Expertise and Process
When you choose MoldGuard Kenya, you are choosing unmatched expertise. Our certified technicians follow a rigorous, proven process to guarantee your property is restored safely:

1. **Advanced Inspection:** Utilizing thermal imaging and moisture meters to locate hidden mould.
2. **Strict Containment:** Establishing negative air pressure zones to prevent cross-contamination during removal.
3. **Safe Remediation:** Removing contaminated materials safely, rather than just bleaching over them.
4. **Air Purification:** Deploying industrial-grade HEPA air scrubbers to remove airborne spores.
5. **Clearance Verification:** Ensuring your space is comprehensively sanitized before we consider the job done.
